About Me

I am a graphic designer with a passion for hacking-inspired aesthetics. My work features a dark color palette, glitch effects, and neon accents, creating an immersive and visually striking experience. With a keen eye for detail and a love for interactive design, I strive to push the boundaries of traditional graphic design and incorporate elements of the hacking culture into my creations. Whether it's designing a captivating portfolio, developing an attention-grabbing website, or crafting dynamic digital assets, I aim to deliver innovative and visually stunning solutions that leave a lasting impression.
